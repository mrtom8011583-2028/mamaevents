## 🔥 **WHAT HAPPENS NOW:**

**When Users Click "Request Quote":**
1. Beautiful dialog opens (800px × 960px)
2. Step 1 shows: Service type selection cards
3. User fills in event details
4. Clicks "Continue" → Step 2
5. Enters contact information
6. Clicks "Continue" → Step 3
7. Selects budget & preferences
8. Clicks "Get Your Free Quote"
9. ✅ Data saves to Firebase `quote_requests` collection
10. ✅ Success notification appears
11. ✅ Dialog closes
12. ✅ Admin can see it in `/admin` dashboard
