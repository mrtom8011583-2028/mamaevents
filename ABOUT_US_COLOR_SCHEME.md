# About Us Page - Color Scheme
## Minimal Gold Touch Strategy

### 🎨 Primary Color Palette
**Main Focus:** White, Grey, and Black

- **White** (#FFFFFF) - Card backgrounds, clean surfaces
- **Soft Grey** (#F5F5F5) - Section backgrounds  
- **Medium Grey** (#888888) - Borders, subtle accents
- **Deep Black** (#121212) - Text, dark cards
- **Charcoal Grey** (#2C2C2C) - Secondary dark elements

### ✨ Gold Usage (Minimal Touches Only)

**WHERE GOLD IS USED:**

1. **Section Labels** - Small uppercase text like "OUR STORY", "CORE VALUES"
   - Minimal visual weight
   - Draws attention to section titles

2. **Decorative Lines** - Small accent lines (60px width x 4px height)
   - Hero section underline
   - Section dividers

3. **Icon Circles** - Core Values and Stats icons
   - 70px circular backgrounds
   - Contains white icons

4. **Stat Numbers** - Counter animation numbers
   - Makes key metrics stand out
   - Limited to 4 cards total

5. **Icon Highlights** - Small icons in Mission/Vision cards
   - Icon color only (not background)
   - Minimal gold glow background

**WHERE GOLD IS NOT USED:**

❌ Large card borders (using grey instead)
❌ Main text bodies (black/grey only)
❌ Background colors (white/grey/black only)  
❌ Full card backgrounds
❌ Button backgrounds (dark with gold gradient only for CTA)

### 📊 Gold Distribution

**Total Gold Elements:** ~12-15 small touches across entire page
**Gold vs Monochrome Ratio:** ~5% gold, 95% white/grey/black

### ✅ Result
A sophisticated **monochromatic design** with **subtle premium accents** that doesn't overwhelm the clean aesthetic.
